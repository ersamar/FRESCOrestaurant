Our Dress Code Policy

We pride ourselves on offering an elegant and refined atmosphere for our guests. To maintain the ambiance, we kindly request adherence to the following dress code:

  • Smart Casual Attire: We encourage stylish, yet comfortable clothing that reflects sophistication.
  • Gentlemen: Collared shirts, tailored pants or jeans, and smart shoes are preferred.
  • Ladies: Dresses, skirts, blouses, or dressy tops paired with elegant footwear are recommended.
  • Not Permitted: Sportswear, athletic gear, flip-flops, excessively casual clothing (e.g., beachwear), or any attire that may disrupt the elegant setting.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. Should you have any questions about our dress code, please don't hesitate to contact us.
